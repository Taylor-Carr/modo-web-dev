HOW DOES IT WORK?

Brief

We’ll start with a quick chat to understand your business, your goals, and the type of customers you want to attract. We’ll also gather everything we need to build the site - pages, content, images, brand colours, and any examples you like.

Build

Once we have a clear plan, we’ll jump straight into building your website. We’ll create the layout, design, and key sections to match your brand and make sure the site is built to convert visitors into customers. When the first draft is ready, we’ll send you a demo link to review.

Review

When you've reviewed the demo site and sent over any changes you’d like. We’ll apply your feedback and keep refining the site until you’re 100% happy and ready to go live.

4

Launch

Once approved, we’ll purchase a domain name and launch your website. Hosting is included for free with no monthly fees, and is renewed on a year-to-year basis to keep things simple and low-risk for everyone. Need updates later? Content edits (text and images) and general maintenance are free - large design changes are billed at our hourly rate.
